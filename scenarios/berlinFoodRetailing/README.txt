### (translation)
- shopfacilities contains all shops and can be read with the MATSim-FacilityReader
- vehicleTypes contains typical vehicles in food retailing industry and can be read with VehicleTypeReader/Loader in contrib/freight
- distributionCenterFac contains all distribution centers and can be read with the MATSim-FacilityReader
- network - network of the Berlin/Brandenburg region
- carrierLEH_v2_withFleet contains the modelled services(demand) of the food retailing carriers (read in with CarrierPlanReader)
- shops.xlsx - all information about shops
- lager.xlsx - all information about distribution centers
- demand_retailer_commodity - daily demand for categories of goods, food retailing an business type.
